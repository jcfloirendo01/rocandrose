The Challenge

Most brand work is cosmetic. It looks different without acting different.

A new logo without a new positioning is just expensive art direction. Real brand work changes how people think about you before they ever see your creative. We start with strategy — who you are, what you stand for, who you're for — before a single typeface is considered. The identity follows the strategy. Every time.

01

Strategy before aesthetics

Positioning, messaging, and audience definition come first. We don't open Figma until we know exactly who the brand is for and what it needs to say.

02

Systems over logos

A logo is one asset. A brand system is everything — colour, type, motion, voice, photography, layout. We build systems that generate consistent output without constant oversight.

03

Built for resilience

We design identities that hold up across contexts — a billboard at 100km/h, a profile picture at 32px, a TikTok watermark. If it doesn't work everywhere, it doesn't work.

What's Included

From naming to system.

01

Naming & Verbal Identity

Brand name development, trademark screening, and verbal identity systems — the words, phrases, and sentence structures that make a brand sound like itself.

02

Visual Identity Systems

Logo, colour system, typography, iconography, illustration style, and layout principles — delivered as a complete system with rules, not just a mood board.

03

Brand Guidelines

Living documentation in Notion or your preferred tool — updated as the brand evolves. Brand guidelines that people actually read and follow.

04

Tone of Voice

Voice pillars, writing principles, channel-specific guidance, and example copy across every major touchpoint. With a "sounds like / doesn't sound like" reference your team will actually use.

05

Sonic Identity

Brand audio signature, sonic mnemonic, and production music direction for video and social. The next frontier of brand recognition — most brands still haven't touched it.

06

Photography & Art Direction

Shot lists, styling direction, model briefs, and on-set art direction from our Elwood studio. We define the visual world before we shoot in it.

How We Work

From research to living system.

Phase 01

Research

Stakeholder immersion, customer interviews, competitive landscape mapping, and cultural audit. We learn what space is available to own before we decide how to own it.

Phase 02

Concept

Two or three fully resolved creative directions — not three half-finished options. Each direction is presented with rationale, not just visuals.

Phase 03

Refine

One chosen direction taken to full resolution across all key applications. Two rounds of structured feedback — enough to get it right, not so many that it becomes design by committee.

Phase 04

System

Guidelines, asset library, Figma component library, and team onboarding. We don't hand over a zip file — we make sure your team can use what we've built.
