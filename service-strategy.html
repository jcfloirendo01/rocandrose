The Challenge

Most marketing plans are built around what's easy to measure. Not what matters.

Brands optimise for the channel with the best attribution, position against the competitor with the loudest ads, and set their pricing strategy based on cost-plus rather than perceived value. Real strategy starts with understanding how your customers actually make decisions — and then building a position that's genuinely impossible to copy. That's a harder problem than most agencies want to tackle. It's the only one we're interested in.

01

Insight before strategy

We don't start with frameworks — we start with customers. Every strategy engagement includes primary research, because the insight that unlocks positioning is almost never in the brief.

02

Position before tactics

Tactics without positioning is noise. A clear position makes every channel decision easier, every creative brief sharper, and every campaign more effective — before a dollar is spent.

03

Fractional leadership, not consulting

We sit inside the business, not outside it. Fractional CMO engagements are monthly, hands-on, and measured against commercial outcomes — not deliverable completion.

What's Included

The thinking behind the work.

01

Positioning & Messaging

Brand positioning statement, messaging hierarchy, proof points, and value proposition — tested with real customers before it's finalised. Not a workshop artifact.

02

Customer Research

Qualitative interviews, quantitative surveys, jobs-to-be-done mapping, and cohort analysis. We talk to your customers so you understand them better than your competitors do.

03

Category Mapping

Competitive landscape analysis, whitespace identification, and category design — including plays for brands that want to define a new category rather than compete in an existing one.

04

GTM Strategy

Go-to-market plans for product launches, market entries, and rebrand activations — with channel mix, budget allocation, timing, and success metrics agreed before execution begins.

05

Annual Planning

Facilitated annual planning sessions that produce a 12-month marketing calendar, channel strategy, and budget framework aligned to business goals — not just marketing KPIs.

06

Fractional CMO Support

A senior strategist embedded in your business for 2–4 days per month — attending leadership meetings, setting channel strategy, and holding the team accountable to the plan.

How We Work

From ambiguity to clarity.

Phase 01

Discover

Stakeholder interviews, customer research, business model analysis, and market landscape mapping. We build a shared understanding of where the brand is before we decide where it should go.

Phase 02

Diagnose

What's working, what isn't, and why. The constraints, the opportunities, and the assumptions that need to be challenged. We identify the one or two things that would change everything — if resolved.

Phase 03

Design

Strategy development — positioning, messaging, channel mix, and 12-month roadmap. Presented to your leadership team with clear rationale and commercial projections.

Phase 04

Deploy

Strategy handed off to the execution team — ours or yours — with a clear brief, measurable milestones, and a 90-day review built in. A strategy that doesn't get executed isn't a strategy.
