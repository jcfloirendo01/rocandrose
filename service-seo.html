The Challenge

Organic search is the highest-intent channel there is. Most brands never unlock it.

They build sites that are hard to crawl, produce content that doesn't match what their customers actually search, and measure success by vanity rankings rather than revenue. We build search programs that compound — where every month of work raises the floor, not just the ceiling.

01

Technical foundation first

Crawlability, indexability, Core Web Vitals — fixed before a single piece of content is written. Bad foundations make good content invisible.

02

Topical authority over keywords

We build content systems around topics, not keyword lists. That's what builds authority in a post-HCU world — and in whatever comes after it.

03

Revenue, not rankings

A page-1 keyword that doesn't drive revenue is a distraction. We track rankings because we have to. We track revenue because we want to.

How We Work

Four phases. Compounding returns.

Phase 01

Audit

Complete technical crawl, keyword universe mapping, competitor gap analysis, and a frank assessment of what's holding your current rankings down.

Phase 02

Strategy

12-month roadmap with prioritised initiatives, content clusters, link-building targets, and revenue projections tied to organic visibility goals.

Phase 03

Execute

Technical fixes shipped, content published on cadence, link opportunities built and earned. We run the program — you stay in the loop monthly.

Phase 04

Compound

Quarterly re-prioritisation based on algorithm updates, new opportunities, and what's working. The program gets smarter every month — so do the returns.
