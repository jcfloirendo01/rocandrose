The Challenge

Most brand video is expensive. And unmeasurable.

Production agencies deliver beautiful work that was never briefed against a commercial objective. The media team receives assets they can't test. The brand team can't explain the ROI. We solve this by putting production and performance in the same room — because the brief that drives the creative should also define how we know if it worked.

01

Brief is the brief

Every brief defines a commercial objective before a creative direction. We don't make beautiful things that don't work — we make things that work, beautifully.

02

Performance is the proof

Every campaign has a hook test, a creative learning agenda, and a post-campaign debrief tied to media performance. We know what worked and why.

03

Speed without compromise

One production day generates content for 6 weeks. Our in-house post team has a 48-hour first-cut SLA and a 5-day delivery guarantee for performance formats.

How We Work

From brief to delivery in days.

Phase 01

Brief

Creative brief development with your team — commercial objective, platform strategy, audience profile, and creative constraints. We don't shoot until the brief is locked.

Phase 02

Pre-Production

Treatment, shot list, talent casting, wardrobe direction, location scouting, and call sheet. Every shoot day is planned to the minute — because studio time is expensive.

Phase 03

Shoot

Director-led production in our Fitzroy studio or on location. One production day generates primary footage for 6–10 deliverables across formats and platforms.

Phase 04

Post

In-house editing, colour grading, music licensing, and final delivery in all required formats within 5 business days. First cut in 48 hours — always.
