The Challenge

Email has the best ROI of any channel. Most brands haven't unlocked it.

They send one broadcast per week, have three automations set up in 2021 that nobody has touched since, and wonder why email accounts for 8% of revenue. When we inherit accounts, we typically find 60–80% of available revenue sitting in flows that don't exist or segments that have never been built. We build the infrastructure to capture it.

01

Segmentation before sends

Sending the same email to every subscriber is the fastest way to destroy deliverability and suppress revenue. Every campaign starts with a segmentation decision.

02

Automation as infrastructure

Flows aren't set-and-forget — they're living infrastructure. We audit, update, and A/B test automations every quarter so they keep earning as the brand evolves.

03

LTV over open rate

Open rate tells you who opened. Revenue per recipient tells you if it worked. We optimise for the latter — and report on it accordingly.

How We Work

From untapped channel to revenue engine.

Phase 01

Audit

Platform audit, deliverability health check, flow map, segment analysis, and a revenue opportunity assessment. We tell you exactly how much you're leaving on the table.

Phase 02

Architecture

Full lifecycle map — every customer touchpoint, every trigger, every segment. Built in your platform of choice with clear revenue attribution per flow.

Phase 03

Build

Flows built, templates designed, lists cleaned, and deliverability warmed — in that order. We launch the highest-value flows first and expand from there.

Phase 04

Optimise

Ongoing A/B testing of subject lines, send times, segments, and flow copy. Monthly reporting on revenue per recipient, LTV by cohort, and list health.
